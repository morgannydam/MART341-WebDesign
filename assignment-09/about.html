About this Instructable

I chose this project because I am a printmaker, so I have the knowledge to instruct others on how to do it. I also really enjoy printmaking and want to share that experience with anyone who wants to learn about it.
Art is an important way of expressing ones self and printmaking is just one form of being able to do this. This instructable shows relief printmaking, but there's also screen printing, etching, lithography, engraving, and more.
If you want to learn more, there are a couple of intro printmaking classes offered at the University of Montana (shown on left) as well as at the Zootown Community Art Center (shown on right) located here in town.
