Biography

Morgan Nydam was born in Corvallis, Oregon on January 3, 1998.
She moved around often as a child, but was mostly raised in Great Falls, Montana. She attended Great Falls High School where she graduated in 2016. Then she went on to study at the University of Montana in Missoula. She began working towards her Bachelors of Art with an Art History Minor. She is expected to graduate on May 1, 2021.
Morgan enjoys many different mediums of art, but her favorites are printmaking photography, and painting.
