The 90's

Pop Culture

Film · Music · Fashion

Film

The first full-length CGI movie, Pixar's Toy Story, is released, revolutionizing animated films.
Titanic becomes a cultural phenomenon throughout the world, and eventually becomes the highest-grossing film of all time, grossing over $1.8 billion worldwide.

Music



The decade was best known for grunge, gangsta rap, R&B, teen pop; eurodance, electronic dance music, and the renewed popularity of punk rock.

Fashion

Trends included:

  • Earth and jewel tones, as well as an array of minimalistic style and design influences
  • The Grunge hype at the beginning of the decade popularized flannel shirts among both sexes during the 1990s
  • Bleached Blond hair became very popular in the late '90s, as was men with short hair with the bangs "flipped up"
